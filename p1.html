What is Corona Virus or COVID-19 Pandemic?
What is Virus Corona?
Coronaviruses are a vast array of viruses thatcan cause disease in both animals and peopleA number of corona viruses are known to cause severity of human respiratory diseases ranging from common coldto more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and severe acute respiratory syndrome ( SARS) The recently discovered Coronavirus causes the disease Covid-19
What is malady of Covid-19?
Covid-19 disease is an infectious disease caused by the Corona virus virus that was last detected This new virus and its disease were not known before it started spreading in Wuhan , China, in December 2019 Covid-19 has now become a pandemic which affects many countries around the world.
